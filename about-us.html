How to Use!

Have you ever craved a food but weren't sure where to find it?

If so, than this site was made for you! -----'s goal is to connect hungry customers with local restaurants. The process is easy! First take a look at the mood board homepage and browse the display of foods until you find the right catagory for you. Once you click on that catagory, you will be brought to a new page containg of all the different food options from that one catagory. Once you find the food that you like you will be brought to a checkout page that will send you over to that restaurants website where you can finish your order. Easy, simple, and fun! Happy eating.
